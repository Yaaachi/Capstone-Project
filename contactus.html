Frequently Asked Questions

Question Answer
Where is Uganda located? Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa, bordered by Kenya, South Sudan, the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), Rwanda, and Tanzania.
How is the security of Uganda? Uganda has invested a lot in security as it is the most tourist attraction amongst other countries
Are the health infrasturectures still in the good state for tourists? Yes, Uganda governance invested alot in Health material including training doctors and Nurses to close that gap.
What is the capital city of Uganda? The capital city is Kampala, which is also the largest city and the commercial hub of the country.
What languages are spoken in Uganda? Official language: English Widely spoken local language: Luganda Other languages include Swahili, Runyankole, Ateso, and many more from over 50 ethnic groups.
